Our vision
The vision behind Cimple is to make Public Sector buying and supplying simple*

We want to save billions to help put more teachers, nurses, police women, social workers back on to the front liner to improve the lives of everyone.All whilst helping businesses and local economies grow* Not much really!

Our Ethos
We believe helping to solve some of the greatest challenges faced by our Schools, Hospitals, Local Authorities etc.is at the heart of why we exist. We believe in a improving traceability, transparency and trust between Public Sector Buyers and their Suppliers and that the strength of the economy going forward will be based on a growing regional economies through Small and Medium Enterprises.

The values that hold us
true and to account.

Simplicity

Things being made beautifully simple are at the heart of everything we do.

Social Good

We believe in making things better for everyone, even if just by a little bit!

Trust

We work on the basis of creating trust which can only be nurtured through authenticity and transparency.
